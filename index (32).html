Text tutorial

Sideloadly is a tool that allows you to install apps on your iPhone or iPad without using the App Store. Here are the steps to use Sideloadly:

1. Download and install Sideloadly on your computer.

2. Connect your iPhone or iPad to your computer using a USB cable.

3. In the Sideloadly window, click on “Add App” to select the IPA file of the app you want to install.

4. Select your device from the list of connected devices in Sideloadly.

5. Click on the “Start” button to start the installation process.

6. Enter your Apple ID and password when prompted to sign the app.

7. Wait for Sideloadly to complete the installation process.

Once the installation process is complete, you will see the app icon on your iPhone or iPad home screen. You can now open the app and use it as you normally would. It is important to note that Sideloadly can only install apps that are not available on the App Store, or that require special permission to install.
